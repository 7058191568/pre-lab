Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

A person who inspires the world

Biography

Dr A.P.J Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th president of india.

Achievements

  • Developed India's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III)
  • Played a major role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Served as the 11th President of India (2002-2007)
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna
